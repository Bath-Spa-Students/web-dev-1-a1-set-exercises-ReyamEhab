Goofs

The night Harry and Marv break in to the house, Kevin sits down and blesses his macaroni and
cheese dinner just before the stroke of nine. This draws obvious attention to his plate. Later, just
before Harry is covered with feathers, you can see a completely different plate with three sections of
food on it.
